Information  about minecraft:-

 Minecraft is often described as a "SandBox Game". This means that it's a virtual land where

users can create their own worlds and experiences, using building blocks, resources discovered

on the site and their own creativity. The game is available on  multiple platforms. Such as

Mobile, Computer and even Consoles. Minecraft has been sold to Microsoft for 2.5 Billion

 

 

You can even build some of your own builds in your own world!  

 

 

 

 

In minecraft you have 3 options, which are:

  • Single Player

  • Multiplayer

  • Realms

I'll be talking about each of the 3 options there are in minecraft.

Single Player- You are allowed to play alone and choose a gamemode of your choice there are "Creative, Survival and Adventure".

In Creative you get access to the whole inventory of minecraft. And you can never die, where as in Survival you will start with nothing and you'll have to work your way out to get stuff, when you get a lot of stuff you can kill the boss also know as "Ender Dragon" if you kill the ender dragon you'll get an achievement for finishing the game!
